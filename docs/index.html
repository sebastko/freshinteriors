About

Welcome to Fresh Interiors, where we believe that beautiful and functional design should be accessible to everyone. We specialize in creating stylish and comfortable spaces that reflect your personal taste and lifestyle. Whether you’re starting with a blank slate or trying to pull together a room that just doesn’t feel quite right, we are here to make the design process simple, stress-free, and - most importantly - tailored to your needs.

My approach is friendly and pressure-free. If you're unsure of what you want, I will guide you. If you have a million ideas, I will help you refine and prioritize them. Worried about making a costly mistake? With my experience, I'll steer you clear of trouble. I'm not here to push trends or upsell services—my goal is simple: to create a space that's a true reflection of you, so at the end of the day, you absolutely love where you live or work. Karolina Kalemba, the Owner of Fresh Interiors

Services

Residential Design

We provide comprehensive interior decorating services that transform your vision into beautifully curated spaces. Our approach focuses on making rooms feel open, inviting, and functional, carefully choosing pieces that suit your needs, style, and budget. We take care of every detail—from space planning and selecting finishes, furniture, window treatments, and lighting to the perfect finishing touches, such as artwork and accessories. Without the hassle of endless shopping trips or second-guessing, you can enjoy a home that feels effortlessly beautiful and uniquely yours.

Paint Consultation

Our color consultations are designed to make choosing the perfect palette simple and inspiring. We work closely with you to understand your style, consider the lighting and flow of your space, and create color combinations that feel both harmonious and personal. Whether you're looking to add a bold pop of color or embrace soft, calming tones, we provide expert guidance to ensure every shade works beautifully together.

Office/Commercial Design

We offer commercial design services tailored for offices and small businesses and create spaces that are both functional and inspiring. We understand that a well-designed workplace goes beyond aesthetics - it needs to enhance customer experience, reinforce brand identity, and support productivity. Whether you're setting up a new office or refreshing an existing space, we work closely with you to craft a design that balances style, comfort, and practicality - ensuring your business leaves a lasting impact on clients and motivates your team.

Beauty Salon Makeover

Multiple umbrellas provide shade, while bold statement planters introduce natural beauty. The result is a vibrant, functional space where residents can unwind, connect, and enjoy a variety of activities.
