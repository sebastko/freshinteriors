About

Welcome to Fresh Interiors, where we believe that beautiful and functional design should be accessible to everyone. We specialize in creating stylish and comfortable spaces that reflect your personal taste and lifestyle. Whether you’re starting with a blank slate, or trying to pull together a room that just doesn’t feel quite right, we are here to make the design process simple, stress-free, and - most importantly - tailored to your needs.

My approach is friendly and no-pressure. If you’re not sure what you want, I will guide you. If you have a million ideas, I will help you to narrow them down. And if you’re worried about making a big costly mistake? I’ve got the experience to steer you clear of trouble. I am not here to push trends or upsell fancy services—I want to make sure that the final result is a reflection of you, and at the end of the day you love where you live or work. Karolina Kalemba, the Owner of Fresh Interiors
